Breakfast Wrap

Description

We all have busy lives which is why i value simplicity when cooking. This is an easy recipe for a delicious breakfast wrap. It's made with easy whole ingredients which will make your grocery trip a breeze. It also can be cooked in less than 15 minutes if you can manage your time right!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Wrap
  • Eggs
  • Turkey Pepperoni
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Provolone Cheese
  • salt
  • Optional: Red Pepper Flakes
