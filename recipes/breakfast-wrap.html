Breakfast Wrap

Description

We all have busy lives which is why i value simplicity when cooking. This is an easy recipe for a delicious breakfast wrap. It's made with easy whole ingredients which will make your grocery trip a breeze. It also can be cooked in less than 15 minutes if you can manage your time right!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Wrap
  • Eggs
  • Turkey Pepperoni
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Provolone Cheese
  • salt
  • Oil
  • Optional: Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Heat up pan in medium heat and add a splash of oil.
  2. While pan is heating up, place down a tortilla wrap and place 2 slices on cheese on the wrap.
  3. Spread cottage cheese on top of the slices cheesed and tortilla wrap.
  4. Place turkey pepperoni on pan and fry until lightly crispy. Then place pepperoni on wrap.
  5. If needed, add additional oil to the pan.
  6. Add 2 eggs on pan and scramble to your desired level. Then place the scrambled eggs on the wrap.
  7. Close the wrap to ensure al the contents stay inside.
  8. Add additional oil to the pan and place wrap into the pan.
  9. Wait until each side is golden brown and place back on your plate to enjoy!
